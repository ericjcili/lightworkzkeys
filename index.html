About Us

Light up the night with Light Workz of the Florida Keys. We specialize in architectural and landscape lighting for residential and hospitality properties, including event spaces, restaurants, hotels, and more, creating beautiful, breathtaking landscape lighting for you and your visitors to enjoy.

Our Florida Keys outdoor lighting company understands that exterior lighting is a crucial part of landscape design. It can enhance the beauty of the landscape and create an atmosphere that is perfect for relaxation and enjoyment. Landscape lighting in the Keys can be used to highlight features of a property, create an inviting atmosphere, or even help deter crime. Light Workz offers a variety of services to meet your needs and budget.

Outdoor lighting can be a daunting task for homeowners. With hot weather and shorter days, it is difficult to keep up with the exterior beauty of your home. However, there are some easy ways to light up your house in the heat. If you have a backyard or patio, you can install lights at the base of trees and shrubs. You can also install lights around your porch or deck railing to illuminate them when they are in use. If you have an outdoor fireplace, then you can light that up too! But don't feel like you have to wait until summer, outdoor lighting is stylish all year round!
